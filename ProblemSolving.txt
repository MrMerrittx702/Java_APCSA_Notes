Stepwise Refinement
> The process of breaking a larger problem into smaller manageable subproblems.
> Each sub problem could then be implemented with a method
> This makes a problem easier to write, reuse, debug, test, modify, and maintain

Benefits
  > Simpler Programs
  > Reuseable Methods
  > Easier Development, Debugging, and Testing.
  > Facilitates Teamwork
